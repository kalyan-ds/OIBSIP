## 📌 Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Oasis Infobyte Data Analytics Internship**.

The objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on retail sales data to uncover patterns, trends, customer behavior, and generate actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Perform Data Cleaning
- Calculate Descriptive Statistics
- Analyze Customer Demographics
- Study Product Sales Patterns
- Perform Time Series Analysis
- Visualize Insights
- Provide Business Recommendations

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Electronics is the highest-selling category.
- Regular customers contribute the largest share.
- Credit Card is the most preferred payment method.
- Excellent customer feedback dominates.
- USA contributes the highest number of customers.
- Medium-income customers form the majority.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase inventory for Electronics products.
- Introduce loyalty programs for regular customers.
- Offer cashback and discounts on card payments.
- Continue improving delivery efficiency.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing regions.
